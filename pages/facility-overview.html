Facilities

Our Facilities are more than just buildings; they are places where residents can thrive, socialize, and enjoy a fulfilling lifestyle.

Dining Rooms

The dining rooms in Horizon Living offer a warm, inviting atmosphere where residents can enjoy nutritious meals in the company of friends, fostering a sense of community and comfort.

Fitness Center

The fitness center is equipped with a variety of machines and tools, providing residents with a space to stay active and maintain their health in a supportive and energizing environment.

Medical Center

The medical center is staffed with compassionate professionals and equipped with modern facilities, ensuring residents have access to personalized care and medical support whenever needed.

Recreational Building

The recreational building offers a vibrant space for residents to engage in hobbies, socialize, and enjoy a variety of activities that promote both mental and physical well-being.
