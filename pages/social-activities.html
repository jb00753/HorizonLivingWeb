Social Activities

Emily Green

Senior Activities Coordinator

Community Involvment

There are multiple activities and groups our residents can participate in that are all carefully planned by by our Activities Coordinator.

Music Night

On Friday nights we host a Karaoke and Dance event where our residents can sing to their hearts desire or take to the dance floor to have a good time with others.
All residents and guests are welcome!

Walking Club

The Walking Club goes on various long and short walks through out the day and residents are welcome to join at any time. Whether they enjoy long walks into the evening or short morning walks to start the day!

Make Your Own Dish

On Wednesdays we host a pottery class where residents can create their own dishes and design them how they like to use in their everyday lives.
All residents are welcome!
